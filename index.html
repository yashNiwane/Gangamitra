Reward System

Working Flow of Reward System

reward system is designed to recognize and celebrate your valuable contributions to our mission while inspiring you to bring more like-minded individuals into our community.

Earning Points through Active Participation

Participants earn points by actively engaging in various activities related to river conservation. This includes participating in quizzes, sharing valuable insights, contributing ideas, and taking actions that promote our cause.

Achievement Levels and Unlocking Rewards

As you accumulate points, you'll progress through different achievement levels. Each level comes with exclusive rewards, such as digital badges, e-certificates, and access to premium content that deepens your understanding of river conservation.

Inviting Friends for Bonus Points

Here's where the magic happens! You can invite friends, family, colleagues, and anyone passionate about river conservation to join our community. When they sign up and actively participate, you earn bonus points.

Our reward system is not just about earning points; it's about making a meaningful impact on our rivers and the environment. By participating actively and inviting others to do the same, you become an integral part of our mission. Together, we can create a brighter future for our rivers and inspire a new generation of environmental champions!
